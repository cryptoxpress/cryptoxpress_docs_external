## Empower Your Connections: Adding Beneficiaries Made Simple!

1. Simply launch the **CryptoXpress App** and head straight to the **More Section** at the bottom right corner.

<center>
<img src={Banner1} width="270" />
</center>

2. In the **More Section** screen Simply tap on your **Profile.**

<center>
<img src={Banner2} width="270" />
</center>

3. In the **Profile**, **Account Links** section tap on the **Saved Addresses** option to open the **Beneficiaries Screen**

<center>
<img src={Banner3} width="270" />
</center>

4. In **Beneficiaries Screen** all the added **Beneficiaries** would be visible and user's will be able to Add a **New Beneficiary**, **Edit the Exisiting one**, or **Remove Beneficiary**.

<center>
<img src={Banner4_1} width="270" />
</center>

5. To add a new Beneficiary tap on the **Add a new Address** button at the bottom. This will open an Prompt.

<center>
<img src={Banner4} width="270" />
</center>

6. In the **Prompt** fill the required Details of the **Beneficiary** such as **Name**, **Wallet Address**, and **Chain**.After Filling in the **Details** just Tap in **Save** Button will save the new **Beneficiary** and it would be now visible in the List.

<center>
    <div className="items-center">
        <img src={Banner5} width="270" style={{"margin" : "5px 10px 5px 5px"}} />
        <img src={Banner6} width="270" style={{"margin" : "5px 5px 5px 10px"}}/>
    </div>
</center>

7. To **Edit details** of any **Beneficiary** tap on the **Edit button** of the corresponding **Beneficiary**, it will open up an **prompt** asking the **new details**, Now to save just tap on **Save button** to finish editing the Details.

8. To **Delete** any **Beneficiary** from the List Simply tap on the **Delete Button** of the **Corresponding Beneficiary**, this will remove the **Beneficiary** from the **List**.
